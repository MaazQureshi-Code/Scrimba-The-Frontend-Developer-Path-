Visit Torshov

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Oslo.

Top three activities to do at Torshov

Visit the park

Take a walk in Torshovparken, a beautiful park with a playground and a pond.

See a play

Visit Torshovteatret, a small theatre with a great atmosphere and interesting plays.

Play sports

Enjoy various sports activities at the local sports facilities in Torshov.
